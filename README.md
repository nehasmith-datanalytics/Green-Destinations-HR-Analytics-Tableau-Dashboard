# Green-Destinations-HR-Analytics-Tableau-Dashboard


## 📝 Project Description
The **Green Destination HR Analytics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of workforce demographics, compensation trends, and attrition patterns. By integrating employee count, age distribution, income levels, and attrition data, the dashboard enables HR leaders to make data‑driven decisions that strengthen recruitment, retention, and succession planning strategies.

This project transforms raw HR data into actionable insights, helping the organization build a resilient, diverse, and future‑ready workforce.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze workforce composition by age and income groups.  
- Measure attrition rates across different demographics.  
- Identify vulnerable segments with high turnover.  
- Evaluate compensation trends and their impact on retention.  
- Support HR strategies for recruitment, pay equity, and succession planning.  

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions (KPIs)
1. **Workforce Demographics**
   - How many employees fall into each age group?  
   - How is the workforce distributed across income brackets?  

2. **Attrition Analysis**
   - Which age groups show the highest attrition rates?  
   - Is attrition concentrated among lower‑income employees?  
   - How does retirement affect senior age groups?  

3. **Compensation Trends**
   - What is the average monthly income by age group?  
   - Do income disparities correlate with attrition?  
   - Are mid‑career employees compensated fairly compared to entry‑level and senior employees?  

4. **Strategic Insights**
   - How balanced is the workforce across career stages?  
   - What HR actions can reduce attrition and improve retention?  

---

## 🔄 Process
1. **Data Collection** – Gather employee records with age, income, and attrition status.  
2. **Data Preparation** – Categorize employees into age and income groups.  
3. **Analysis** – Compute employee counts, average monthly income, and attrition rates.  
4. **Visualization** – Build Tableau dashboards showing:  
   - Employee count by age group (with attrition).  
   - Average monthly income by age group (with attrition).  
   - Employee count by income group (with attrition).  
5. **Insights** – Identify trends and recommend HR actions.  
6. **Review & Optimization** – Validate dashboard accuracy and share with stakeholders.  

---

## 📈 Insights Example
- **20–30 age group**: Lower income, higher attrition → need stronger entry‑level retention programs.  
- **31–40 age group**: Largest workforce share, moderate attrition → stable mid‑career talent pool.  
- **41–50 age group**: Rising attrition despite higher income → potential career stagnation issues.  
- **51+ age group**: Attrition mainly retirement‑driven → succession planning required.  
- **<5K income group**: Smaller workforce but higher attrition → pay dissatisfaction risk.  
- **15K+ income group**: Lowest attrition, but external opportunities may drive turnover.
